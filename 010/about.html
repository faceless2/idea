Make Tree Apps first-class citizens

This is just a proof of concept, but in theory you could have (probably) a subset of tree apps here, one on each tab. I suggest a subset because some (ie one name tree) are not directly related to an individual, so may not make much sense when you have the biography of an individual below. But many (SlippyTree, Family Timeline etc) could work quite well here.

Other approaches could be an iframe rather than adding directly to the page, and there could be a "plus" tab which allows a user to choose other apps, or to select which apps they want on tabs.
